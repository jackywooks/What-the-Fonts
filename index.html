其實，你喜歡一個人，就像喜歡富士山。你可以看到它，但不能搬走它。你有甚麼方法可以移動一座富士山？答案是，你自己走過去。愛情也是如此，逛過就已經足夠

Loving a person is like loving mountain Fuji. You can look at it but you cannot move it. Is there any way to move Mountain Fuji? The answer is, you can only walk toward it. So is Love, it is enough when you have experienced it.
